StockX is the biggest sneakers exchange online (offline too I believe). Many sneakers resellers use this platform to sell limited edition sneakers that they have managed to put their hands on. I came across a dataset they publised on kaggle last year (2019). The dataset itself doesn't containt to much info. It is just some basic info of each transaction that has been made on their platform. The problem with this kind of info is that I can only glance at what has been sold and not what hasn't been sold which I believe is also very important.

Anyways, My thinking was, maybe, just maybe, the data they have published could be an indicator to an overall performance of the company , or more specifically, company stock ? I wouldn't have high hopes on it but I thought I should give it a try nonetheless.

So the dataset contains info regarding 50 different sneakers of three brands; adidas, nike and air jordan. Air Jordan is actually part of nike.
